Context 

      While coming up with ideas for my story map, I was trying to figure out what kind of story I could tell with a progressing narrative. I ended up creating my story map based around marijuana’s legalization status and the tax revenue it earns in the United States. This was something I wanted to learn more about considering there has been so many legalization efforts over the last few years. I found the current progress of legalization efforts to be very fascinating and the amount of money that some states were making blew my mind. “Unrealized Economic Opportunity” is how I keep up with current events unfolding here in the U.S.
