A Night Out 

Context 

      I found this project to be especially pleasing to work on as it was the first time utilizing Mapbox Studio. The main focus was creating an aesthetic and aesthetic cohesion. I was inspired by a film I had recently seen and tried to create a map involving a premise similar to that of the movie. My map was focused on a night out that could last forever. I filled my map with icons I designed in Illustrator and created a dynamic color palette that ranged from sun up to sun down as you zoomed in.
